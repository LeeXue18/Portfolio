Hidden Pulse

AR Design

This is an AR storytelling project revealing the secrets that are buried under the land of hunter's point. As used to be a shipyard, hunter's point was once used as nuclear experiment site. Although nowadays it is claimed to be safe and clear, many people living there still suffer from diseases or cancer that "comes from nowhere".

Hidden Pulse guides users through the story of that land by the "current resident" Marcus, and reveals the "truth" underneath the illusion. From the story, users gain a knowledge of what has happened, and reflects on the so-called status quo.
