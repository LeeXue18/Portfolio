Hidden Pulse

AR Design

An augmented reality narrative experience that uncovers the hidden environmental history of Hunter's Point, San Francisco. Once a thriving naval shipyard, this site was later used for radiological testing — a legacy of contamination that continues to affect residents today, despite official assurances of safety.

Hidden Pulse guides users through this layered history via the perspective of Marcus, a fictional current resident. The experience peels back the surface of everyday life to reveal the complex reality beneath — prompting reflection on environmental justice, institutional transparency, and community resilience.
